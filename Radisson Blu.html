Tresind

A modernist Indian luxury dining experience, is what you can expect, as you enter the first outpost of this popular restaurant in India. The off-white walls and design elements in earthy tones, with water, as the underlying theme, spell sophistication all the way. Corporate Chef Himanshu Saini has skilfully crafted a culinary experience that engages all the senses. The menu showcases traditional dishes from the sub-continent, encompassing the various regional culinary influences.
