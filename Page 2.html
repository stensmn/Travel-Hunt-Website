HOTELS IN MUMBAI

TAJ HOTEL

Taj Hotels is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited, headquartered at Express Towers, Nariman Point, Mumbai. Incorporated by the founder of the Tata Group, Jamsetji Tata, in 1903, the company is a part of the Tata Group, one of India's largest business conglomerates.

THE TRIDENT

Trident Hotels in Mumbai, Hyderabad and Chennai feature Trident Club Rooms and Suites, that are designed for discerning business travellers. Located on the Trident Club Floors, these rooms offer a comfortable and efficient environment, with a host of exclusive Trident Club privileges that will make your stay convenient and memorable. Trident Club privileges include airport transfers, butler service, a private club reception for express check-in and check-out, and access to the Trident Club Lounge for daily breakfast and cocktail hour.

SAHARA STAR

Fronted by Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, this high-end airport hotel is 5 km from both Juhu Beach and the 1st-century Jogeshwari Caves.Refined rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and marble bathrooms. Sleek suites add separate living areas. Some have balconies, glass ceilings, kitchenettes and/or private elevators..

RADISSON BLU

Radisson Blu is an international chain of hotels operated by Radisson Hotels. With roots dating back to the 1960s, the Radisson Blu brand name came into existence in 2009 with a rebranding from Radisson SAS. Its hotels are found in major cities, key airport gateways and leisure destinations.

TAJ LAND ENDS

Traditional rooms with sea views feature Wi-Fi, desks, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Club-level rooms and suites provide butler service and access to a lounge with free breakfast and evening cocktails. Plush suites add separate living rooms, and some offer whirlpool tubs

THE OBEROI

On the shores of Back Bay, this luxe hotel in the Nariman Point business district is 1 km from Wankhede Stadium and 2 km from the Gateway of India arch.

ITC MARATHA

The polished rooms and suites come with free Wi-Fi, TVs, and tea and coffeemakers. Upgraded quarters add butler service, private lounge access and/or massage chairs. Room service is available 24/7.There are multiple restaurant options, including fine dining, a 24-hour eatery, a chic bar and a tearoom. Other amenities consist of an outdoor pool, a spa and a gym.

NOVOTEL

The modern rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and desks, along with minibars, microwaves, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms provide access to a lounge with free beverages, while suites add separate living rooms. Room service is available.The hotel offers free parking and breakfast. Additional amenities include 5 dining options, some with outdoor seating, as well as 2 bars with live music. There's also an outdoor pool and a gym, plus a spa area with a sauna and massage treatments.

FOUR SEASONS

Set 3 km from the Siddhivinayak Temple, this plush high-rise hotel with a glass facade is 8 km from both Girgaum Chowpatty beach and Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Mandai market.Contemporary rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows feature flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and safes, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms add sea views. Suites include living rooms. Room service is available.Free amenities include breakfast and parking. There's an upscale Pan-Asian restaurant, a Mediterranean eatery, and a rooftop pool with a terrace. Other amenities include a gym and a spa, plus a business centre and event space.

ITC GRAND CENTRAL

This polished hotel with British Colonial-inspired architecture is 3 km from the gold roof at the Hindu Siddhivinayak Temple and 10 km from the ornate Gateway of India triumphal arch.Refined rooms feature flat-screen TVs, leather chairs, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms add sitting areas. A women-only floor is available. Room service and butler service are offered 24/7.Parking, laundry service and breakfast are free. There are several swanky restaurants and bars, as well as a spa with an indoor pool and a fitness room. .

KOHINOOR CONTINENTAL

This high-end, contemporary hotel is a 3-minute walk from a metro station, and 5 km from both Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport and the Nehru Science Centre.The refined, modern rooms offer cool colour schemes, free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, as well as minibars. Upgraded rooms add plush sitting areas, while suites offer separate bedrooms and lavish bathrooms. Room service is available 24/7.The hotel has an elegant restaurant, a coffee shop and a cocktail bar. Other amenities include an outdoor pool and a fitness room, plus a business centre and conference facilities.

THE LEELA

A 7-minute walk from a metro station, this upmarket hotel is also 5 km from Mahakali Caves and 16 km from Siddhivinayak Temple.Plush rooms and suites have flat-screens, Wi-Fi (fee), and tea and coffeemakers. Suites add separate living/dining areas, and some have whirlpool tubs or kitchenettes. Some rooms offer access to a lounge offering free breakfast and cocktails. Room service is available.There are sophisticated eateries for Indian, Chinese and French/Italian cuisine, as well a chic lounge, a modern outdoor bar and a 24-hour restaurant. Other amenities include an outdoor pool, a gym and a salon, plus a high-end spa, boutiques, and meeting and event space.
