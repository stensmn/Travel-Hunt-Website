Restaurants near Sahara Star

The Earth Plate

If you are looking for a fantastic place to have a memorable dining experience with excellent food to dine with family and friends, in this part of Mumbai, then The Earth Plate is your ultimate food destination.This spacious all-day dining restaurant has gained immense popularity for sumptuous Sunday brunches and lavish spread of breakfast, with eclectic global cuisine in elegant surroundings. The contemporary décor and opulent interiors definitely, offers a wonderful dining option.

Namak

Namak is one of the many dining options available at Sahara Star Hotel. As the name suggests, this restaurant serves Indian cuisine with dishes from all over the country. Primarily focuses on North Indian food. The restaurant overlooks the garden area, they have open kitchen where you can see chefs whizzing about preparing your meal. The decor is as simple as its name and is made up of earthy tones.

East Asian Spice Trail

This Asian inspired restaurant is cleverly named East which is an abbreviation for East Asian Spice Trail. The menu features pan Asian cuisine and has many live counters and even a Teppanyaki one. The restaurant décor is dark with all black interiors, giving it a very contemporary feel. The service at the restaurant is excellent, they make it a point to be courteous without being intrusive. While the menu includes dishes from the Far East, it is mostly Chinese and Thai that rule the menu.

Mabruk

Other than kebabs and shawramas Middle Eastern cuisine is surprisingly lacking in popularity. It is a huge misconception that this glorious cuisine is all about these two dishes, however, it is much more. Few restaurants try to attempt exploring Middle Eastern fare transforming it into a more mainstream affair. One of those few is ‘Mabruk’, a gorgeous fine dining restaurant dedicated to discovering the delights of Middle Eastern food with finesse and love.
